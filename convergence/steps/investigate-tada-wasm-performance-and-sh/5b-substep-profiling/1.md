### Key architectural finding
The decoder (`decode_audio`) is a single monolithic candle CPU call — no per-frame loop exists in Rust or JS. `decode_ms / frame_count` gives a derived per-frame metric only. True per-frame decode profiling would require modifying the Rust decoder to iterate frames; deferred.

### WASM per-step stats (fox phrase, ex01 voice, cfg=1.6, WASM WebGPU)
- step count: 59 (54 content + trailing 0ms sentinel)
- mean: 248.7ms / median: 74.9ms / p95: 675.8ms / total: 14671ms
- **3-tier structure**:
  - Tier 1 FAST (7-10ms, 18 steps): pre-shift + LLM-only (no VibeVoice)
  - Tier 2 MED (60-110ms, 21 steps): LLM + VV voice-guided steps (voice-prompted phase)
  - Tier 3 SLOW (660-825ms, 19 steps): LLM + VV autoregressive (AR phase, no voice conditioning)
- AR VV step average: ~681ms; voice-guided VV: ~73ms — 9.3x jump between phases

### WASM per-frame decode stats
- frame count: 160 (3200ms audio @ 50fps)
- decode total: 6335ms
- mean frame_ms: 39.6ms (derived, not loop-measured)

### Native CLI per-step stats (TADA_PROFILE_STEPS=1, Var-C Metal)
- gen total: 7.0s (7011ms), step count: 54
- mean: 129.8ms / median: 127.6ms / p95: 137.2ms
- Note: native AR steps (39-52) are steady ~131ms

### Native per-frame decode
- decode total: 1.6s, audio: 1.94s
- frame count: ~97 (@ 50fps)
- mean frame_ms: ~16.5ms

### WASM / native ratios (per-substep)
| Phase | WASM | Native | Ratio |
|---|---|---|---|
| Voice-guided VV step | ~73ms | ~127ms | 0.57x (WASM faster??) |
| AR VV step | ~681ms | ~131ms | **5.1x** |
| Decode frame (derived) | 39.6ms | 16.5ms | **2.4x** |

Note: WASM voice-guided steps being faster than native is surprising — likely because native Metal VV runs full flow-matching while WASM WebGPU VV may take a faster path during voice phase (different branch in `solve_flow_matching_burn`). Further investigation needed.

### Key qualitative finding
**The AR VibeVoice step is the dominant bottleneck at 5.1x WASM/native**. The 19 AR VV steps (660-825ms each) account for ~12.8s of the 14.7s gen budget. Decode is a secondary lever (2.4x, contributing ~6.3s). Optimizing AR VibeVoice WebGPU performance (shader dispatch, memory layout, or batching) would have the highest impact on overall RTF.
